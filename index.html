About us

ACOPPS and AMOPPA is a co-operative registered by the Ghana Registrar General’s Department as ACOPPS AND AMOPPA ORGANIC PRODUCERS AND EXPORTERS LIMITED in 2015. ACOPPS and AMOPPA operate in the Central Region of Ghana, with its head office in Ekumfi Essuehyia in the Ekumfi District, about 60km from its Regional capital Cape Coast. It started with thirteen individual farmers -7 Males and 6 females- cultivating conventional pineapple, on a total of 9 acres of land, for the local market.

LEARN MORE ABOUT ACOPPS & AMOPPA

The xx staff members of ACOPPS and AMOPPA collaborate to carry out our aim of enhancing farmer lives. We work very hard to uphold our independence and offer our members the best services. ACOPPS and AMOPPA view eradicating poverty and ensuring that each member has access to a living wage as their primary priorities.

Because of our direct trade connections, pineapple enthusiasts all over the world like ACOPPS and AMOPPA goods.

Mission

To expand our current level of production of 800metric tons to 2000metric tons through membership empowerment, as an overall objective to raise the living standard of members from its current state to a more sustainable livelihood by the year 2030

Vision

To be the leading organic pineapple producers Co-operative in West Africa region and achieve a sustainable market for pineapple
